# Google Vertex AI Overview

## Summary

Google Vertex AI is a fully managed, unified AI development platform on Google Cloud for building, deploying, and managing generative and predictive AI solutions at scale. It provides access to Gemini models, a wide variety of foundation models, and tools for training, tuning, and deploying machine learning models.

## Content

### What is Google Vertex AI?

Vertex AI enables organizations to build, customize, and deploy AI-powered applications and agents. It supports the full lifecycle of AI solutions, including data preparation, model training, evaluation, deployment, and monitoring, all integrated with Google Cloud's infrastructure.

### Key Features

- Access to Gemini, Imagen, Chirp, Veo, Claude, Gemma, Llama, and other models
- Vertex AI Studio for prompt engineering and model testing
- Model Garden for discovering and customizing models
- MLOps tools for automation, standardization, and management
- Vertex AI Agent Builder for building and scaling enterprise agents
- Integration with BigQuery and other Google Cloud services

### Use Cases

- Building generative AI applications
- Creating and managing enterprise AI agents
- Automating data extraction, analysis, and business workflows
- Rapid prototyping and deployment of ML models

### Privacy and Security

- Built on Google Cloud's secure infrastructure
- Enterprise-grade security and compliance
